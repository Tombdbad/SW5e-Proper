# SW5E Starships

## STARFIGHTERS

### T-65B X-wing Starfighter
**Huge vehicle (starship), unaligned**
- **Armor Class:** 15
- **Hull Points:** 120 (10d12 + 50)
- **Shield Points:** 25
- **Speed:** fly 120 ft. (12 squares in space)
- **Crew:** 1 pilot, 1 astromech droid
- **Passengers:** 0
- **Cargo Capacity:** 110 lb.
- **Consumables:** 1 week
- **Hyperdrive:** Class 1
- **Systems:** Class 2 sensor array, military comm system, S-foils, proton torpedo launcher, targeting computer

**Starfighter.** The X-wing is designed for space combat and atmospheric operations.

**S-Foils.** As a bonus action, the pilot can deploy or retract the S-foils. When deployed, the X-wing's weapons deal additional damage, but its maneuverability is reduced (fly speed reduced to 100 ft.). When retracted, the X-wing can't use its proton torpedoes.

**Astromech Socket.** An astromech droid can interface with the X-wing's systems, providing assistance with repairs, navigation calculations, and targeting.

**Actions:**
- **Quad Laser Cannons.** Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 500/2,000 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d10) energy damage. With S-foils deployed, damage increases to 27 (5d10) energy damage.
- **Proton Torpedoes (8 Torpedoes).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 1,000/4,000 ft., one target. Hit: 33 (6d10) force damage. The proton torpedoes can only be used with S-foils deployed.

**Gargantuan vehicle (starship), unaligned**
- **Armor Class:** 14
- **Hull Points:** 200 (16d20 + 32)
- **Shield Points:** 50
- **Speed:** fly 100 ft. (10 squares in space)
- **Crew:** 2 (pilot, co-pilot), up to 2 gunners
- **Passengers:** 6
- **Cargo Capacity:** 100 tons
- **Consumables:** 2 months
- **Hyperdrive:** Class 0.5 (modified)
- **Systems:** Class 2 sensor array, comm system, concealed compartments, dorsal and ventral turrets

**Modified Freighter.** The YT-1300 is a highly customizable light freighter often modified for increased performance.

**Turret Gunners.** The dorsal and ventral turrets can be operated by gunners or by the pilot and co-pilot with a -2 penalty to attack rolls.

**Concealed Compartments.** The freighter has hidden compartments that can be found with a successful DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check.

**Actions:**
- **Laser Cannons (Turret).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 500/2,000 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d10) energy damage.
- **Concussion Missiles (6 Missiles).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 1,000/4,000 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (5d10) force damage.

## CAPITAL SHIPS

### Nebulon-B Frigate
**Colossal vehicle (starship), unaligned**
- **Armor Class:** 17
- **Hull Points:** 800 (32d20 + 160)
- **Shield Points:** 200
- **Speed:** fly 60 ft. (6 squares in space)
- **Crew:** 920
- **Passengers:** 75
- **Cargo Capacity:** 6,000 tons
- **Consumables:** 2 years
- **Hyperdrive:** Class 2
- **Systems:** Class 1 sensor array, military comm system, 12 TIE Fighter complement, medical bay, 8 escape pods

**Medical Frigate.** The Nebulon-B can be configured as a medical frigate with advanced medical facilities.

**Fighter Complement.** The Nebulon-B can carry and launch up to 12 TIE Fighters or other small starfighters.

**Command Ship.** The Nebulon-B can coordinate fleet operations and serve as a command center.

**Multi-Section Design.** The Nebulon-B has a distinctive design with a forward section connected to an aft section by a thin connecting spar. If the spar takes 100 or more damage from a single attack, the ship is split in two, with each section operating at severely reduced capacity.

**Actions:**
- **Turbolaser Batteries (12).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 3,000/12,000 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (5d10) energy damage.
- **Ion Cannon Batteries (12).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 2,000/8,000 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d10) ion damage. If the target is a vehicle, it must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or be disabled for 1 minute.
- **Tractor Beam Projectors (2).** The frigate targets one Huge or smaller starship within 600 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 60 feet toward the frigate and have its speed reduced to 0 until the start of the frigate's next turn.

### Imperial Star Destroyer
**Colossal vehicle (starship), unaligned**
- **Armor Class:** 20
- **Hull Points:** 2,000 (40d20 + 800)
- **Shield Points:** 500
- **Speed:** fly 40 ft. (4 squares in space)
- **Crew:** 37,000
- **Passengers:** 9,700
- **Cargo Capacity:** 36,000 tons
- **Consumables:** 6 years
- **Hyperdrive:** Class 2
- **Systems:** Class 1 sensor array, military comm system, 72 TIE Fighter complement, ground assault forces, 8 AT-ATs, 20 AT-STs, 30 landing craft, 50 escape pods

**Imperial Capital Ship.** The Star Destroyer is the backbone of the Imperial Navy.

**Fighter Complement.** The Star Destroyer carries a full complement of 72 TIE Fighters, including standard TIEs, Interceptors, and Bombers.

**Ground Assault Forces.** The Star Destroyer carries troops, vehicles, and landing craft for planetary assault operations.

**Command Ship.** The Star Destroyer serves as a mobile command center for Imperial operations.

**Actions:**
- **Heavy Turbolaser Batteries (60).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, range 5,000/20,000 ft., one target. Hit: 44 (8d10) energy damage.
- **Ion Cannon Batteries (60).** Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, range 3,000/12,000 ft., one target. Hit: 33 (6d10) ion damage. If the target is a vehicle, it must succeed on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw or be disabled for 1 minute.
- **Tractor Beam Projectors (10).** The Star Destroyer targets one Gargantuan or smaller starship within 1,200 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 19 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 120 feet toward the Star Destroyer and have its speed reduced to 0 until the start of the Star Destroyer's next turn.
