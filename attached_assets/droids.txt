## ASTROMECH DROIDS

### R-Series Astromech Droid
**Small droid, unaligned**
- **Armor Class:** 13 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points:** 22 (5d6 + 5)
- **Speed:** 20 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 13 (+1) | 8 (-1) |

- **Skills:** Investigation +4, Perception +3, Technology +4
- **Damage Vulnerabilities:** ion
- **Damage Resistances:** necrotic, poison, psychic
- **Condition Immunities:** charmed, exhaustion, poisoned
- **Senses:** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
- **Languages:** Binary, understands Basic but can't speak it
- **Challenge Rating:** 1/4 (50 XP)

**Tool Proficiency.** The droid is proficient with astrotech's implements and can add double its proficiency bonus to checks made with them.

**Starship Systems Interface.** The droid can interface with a starship's computer system and make Technology checks to assist in repairs, navigation calculations, and operations with advantage.

**Actions:**
- **Electroshock Probe.** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) lightning damage.
- **Emergency Repair (Recharge 5-6).** The droid repairs damage to a construct, droid, starship, or vehicle. The target regains 10 (3d6) hit points.
- **Oil Slick (1/day).** The droid releases oil in a 10-foot square centered on itself. This area becomes difficult terrain, and creatures that enter the area or start their turn there must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or fall prone.
